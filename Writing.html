Writing

Since reading is one of Crystal Jhoy Banzil's hobby, she then learned to love writing. For her, creating her own story, building a universe on which she only knew, and to make different flexible characters are fascinating.
As she writes and shares her stories, she slowly discovers the fun on knowing the readers' reactions to her plot twists.
In the present, Crystal is writing on different online writing platforms such as Wattpad and Patreon.
She uses the pen name Clear Shiny and Golden Star Crown. Below are her pen names and works.
