Biography of Crystal Jhoy Banzil

Early Life

Crystal Jhoy Banzil was born on March 30, 2001, as a premature baby, entering the world seven months into her mother's pregnancy. Her fragile condition led to a challenging start to life, with one of her lungs being weak. The doctors faced a critical 50-50 situation as they fought to save her life, requiring assistance for her breathing. Despite the odds, she survived the ordeal, albeit losing her sight due to a diagnosis of Retinopathy of Prematurity (ROP) caused by the oxygen treatment.

Childhood

Growing up, Crystal's family strived to provide her with a happy and ordinary childhood, ensuring she was not treated differently. She enjoyed playing outdoors with her cousins and childhood friends, engaging in activities like running and playing despite any injuries. Her family's support and inclusive approach allowed her to experience a fulfilling childhood.

Education Journey

Elementary

Enrolled at Gen. Maximino Hizon Elementary School, Crystal's education took a pivotal turn when she entered a special education program. Here, she learned essential skills such as reading and writing using the Braille system, utilizing an abacus, a cane, and other tools tailored for individuals with visual impairments. Despite facing academic challenges, bullying, peer pressure, and a mix of teachers, she graduated elementary school with flying colors.

Junior High School

Transitioning to Ramon Magsaysay High School for her junior high education, Crystal's world expanded as she encountered a diverse array of friends and foes. This period marked a time of self-discovery, where she explored various interests, nurtured her dreams, and engaged in hobbies like reading and writing.

Senior High School

Continuing her academic journey at Arellano University for senior high school, Crystal took significant strides towards her path to success. This phase brought her closer to realizing her aspirations, honing her skills, and embracing new opportunities for growth and development.

Current Pursuits

Currently a third-year college student at the College of Mary Immaculate, Crystal remains dedicated to overcoming challenges and pursuing her dreams. With resilience and determination, she navigates the complexities of higher education, striving to achieve her goals and carve out a bright future for herself.
